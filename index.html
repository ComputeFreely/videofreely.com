Video converter

        
Convert media without uploading it.

        
Video Freely is a free browser video converter and audio converter powered by FFmpeg WebAssembly. It can convert to MP4, WebM, GIF, MP3, M4A, WAV, and JPG still frames while keeping selected files on your device.

Common formats

            
Export MP4, WebM, animated GIF, MP3, M4A, WAV, or a JPG frame. Use presets for common jobs or advanced mode for a custom FFmpeg command.

          

          

            
Local processing

            
The selected media file stays in the browser. The app does not require an upload queue, cloud conversion job, account, or final download gate.

          

          

            
Size and clip controls

            
Trim start and end time, mute audio, change bitrate, limit width, reduce frame rate, and adjust output quality before downloading the converted file.

          

        

        

          

            
Does Video Freely upload videos?

            
No. Video Freely runs FFmpeg WebAssembly in the browser and keeps selected media on your device.

          

          

            
What can Video Freely convert?

            
Video Freely can export MP4, WebM, GIF, MP3, M4A, WAV, and JPG still frames from common video and audio inputs.

          

          

            
Why can browser video conversion be slow?

            
Conversion depends on browser memory, CPU speed, input size, and WebAssembly runtime limits because the work happens locally instead of on a server.
